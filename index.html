Sacred Lore & Rules

The Ancient Trial

Long ago, when sages and demons battled for dominion over heaven and earth, the Great Sage Equaling Heaven devised the ultimate trial — a sacred three-by-three grid upon which fate itself would be decided. Victory demands strategy, while defeat demands blood and an inevitable fate of death! The heavens do not crown kings—they fear them. However, a thousand seals cannot bind a single will that refuses order. Strike without pattern. Think without chains. If they can predict you, you are already defeated. Forget not that victory is the moment the heavens stop correcting you!

Sacred Rules of Combat

I.Each warrior enters battle bearing 3 Soul Crystals — the embodiment of their lifeforce and divine resolve.
II.Claim three squares in a row — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal — upon the sacred grid to win the round and wound your foe.
III.The vanquished warrior loses one Soul Crystal per round defeat. When all three shatter, the match ends in execution.
IV.A Clash — when the grid fills with no victor — costs BOTH warriors one Soul Crystal. Mutual exhaustion is its own punishment.
V.Each warrior has 10 seconds to act each turn. Hesitation invites fate's hand to move for you — or forfeit the moment entirely.
VI.When a warrior's last Crystal shatters, the victor unleashes the Ultimate Strike. The defeated vanishes from the field.

The Sacred Markers

Player I claims territory with the Golden Circlet — the very crown that once bound and tormented the Great Sage, now a symbol of his mastery. Player II plants the Lotus Bloom — enlightenment rising from the mud and blood of battle.

The Adversaries

Macaque strikes randomly, unpredictably, like a chaos demon with no strategy. Yaoguai King plays with cunning — blocking your paths, seizing obvious wins. Erlang Shen, the True Lord of the Heavens with his third eye, calculates every possible future using the ancient Minimax oracle. He cannot be beaten — only tied by equal destiny.

Sacred Inspiration

Born from devotion to Black Myth: Wukong — an epic action RPG retelling the mythological Journey to the West. All characters, lore, and aesthetics draw inspiration from the immortal legend of Sun Wukong, the Great Sage Equaling Heaven.
